Negotiation AI  Apr 2023 - Present

A platform to build and play AI negotiation simulations. Used by 750+ users including world-class professors from HBS, Yale, Columbia, and more. Total revenue of $1600+ with 600+ total hours of play time. Uses GPT-4, NextJS, Tailwind, and Supabase. This is the site and the most popular FBI Hostage Negotiation.

Election2033  May 2023

An experimental, AI-powered election game. Players craft tweet-sized campaigns and are elected by a panel of ChatGPT-powered judges (Elon Musk, Snoop Dogg, a sentient goldfish, and more). Built this with a friend and two others at Paradigm using NextJS, Tailwind, and Supabase. Over 200,000 matches were played by 600+ users (people were constantly finding exploits!). You can play here.
